What is VitaeDev?

VitaeDev is a web development team that focuses on community organizations in the Greater Boston Area.

Are you looking to improve your website or to make one entirely new?

Get in contact with us, and we will make sure that the ideas you have for your website will come to life.

Is your website in need of change, but you don't know what you want? Our team will work alongside you to figure something out.

The best part? VitaeDev doesn't cost a single penny for community organizations.

Our team is passionate about our community and wishes to support any all community-oriented organizations in the Greater Boston Area, free of charge.

If you aren't a community organization, contact us anyway, and we'll discuss your project and agree on a reasonable price.
