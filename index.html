COMMUNITY

VitaeDev is a web design & development team located in the Boston area. We're dedicated to helping our community by building modern, appealing websites for nonprofits.

CUSTOM DESIGN

Our websites are hand-crafted to suit your specific tastes & needs, whether that means improving an existing design, backend integration, or building a brand-new site from scratch.

EDUCATION

To make web development accessible to all, we welcome students of all levels and provide mentorship & guided projects to newcomers. Join the team, or check out our free online resources.

The best part? Local non-profits get our services for free.

In the spirit of supporting our community, we develop for non-profit community organizations in the Greater Boston Area free of charge.

Not a non-profit? Contact us anyway, and we'll work out an affordable price.
