What is VitaeDev?

VitaeDev is a web development team that focuses on community organizations in the Greater Boston Area.

Are you a community organization located in the Greater Boston Area looking to improve a website or to make a new website?

Get in contact with us, and we will make sure that the ideas that you have for your website will come to life.

Is your website in need of change, but you don't know what you want? That's ok, our team will work along side of you to figure something out.

The best part? VitaeDev doesn't cost a single penny for community organizations.

Our team is passionate about our community, and wishes to support any community organization in the Greater Boston Area free of charge. If you aren't a community organization, contact us anyway, and we will work with you to figure out reasonable pricing.
